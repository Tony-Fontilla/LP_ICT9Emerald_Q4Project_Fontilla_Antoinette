CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Crisostomo Ibarra)
  • He is a wealthy man who recently returned from the Philippines after studying for seven years in Europe.
  • He seeks to establish a proper school in the Philippines and educate the youth.
  • He is Maria Clara's fiance and the son of Don Rafael.
Maria Clara
  • She is a wealthy woman who studied in a convent for seven years while Ibarra was away.
  • She is Ibarra's fiance.
  • Though it is believed her father is Kapitan Tiago, she is actually Padre Damaso's biological child.
Padre Damaso
  • He is a Franciscan priest who serves as the main antagonist of the novel, having a grudge against the Ibarras.
  • Despite living among the Filipinos for almost two decades, he still sees them as lower and as 'Indios'.
  • He is physically abusive and has committed ruthless acts to Don Rafael, as well as opposing Maria Clara and Crisostomo's marriage.
Padre Salvi
  • He was the one who replaced Padre Damaso as the priest of San Diego.
  • He is said to be incredibly sickly and is greedy towards the people.
  • He also possesses a rivalry with the Alperes which has caused secondhand harm to their people.
Alperes
  • He is the captain of the Gwardiya Sibil and the husband of Donya Consolacion.
  • He butts heads with Padre Salvi and often attempts to insult him.
Donya Consolacion
  • She is the wife of the Alperes, and was formerly a mere 'labandera'.
  • She prefers to speak Spanish despite not even being fluent in it, and she wears too much makeup in a sad attempt to look beautiful.
  • She's also extremely classist.
Tiya Isabel
  • She is Kapitan Tiago's cousin and (technically) Maria Clara's aunt.
  • She was the one who primarily raised Maria Clara.
Kapitan Tiago
  • He became the richest man in San Diego after Don Rafael's passing.
  • He is the husband of Dona Pia Alba and the father of Maria Clara.
  • He often sucks up to the priests and obeys all of their commands.
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra, and died even before the novel began.
  • He was framed by Padre Damaso for murdering the tax collector, merely because he refused to do confession.
  • He died due to sickness while he was still in his prison cell.
  • Like his son, he was against the church and and wanted to establish a school in the Philippines.
Sisa
  • She is the mother of Crispin and Basilio.
  • She was a caring woman, as well as a loving mother, but she was also submissive to the abuse of her husband.
  • When her sons went missing, she succumbed to insanity while attempting to search for them.
Basilio
  • He is the son of Sisa and the older brother of Crispin.
  • When he and his brother were accused of stealing two gold pieces, Basilio fled while Crispin was being beaten by the sacristan mayor.
  • After multiple events, he was taken in by the De Dios family for two months, and witnessed his mother die in front of him with only some lucidity left.
Crispin
  • He is the other son of Sisa and the younger brother of Basilio.
  • While Basilio fled, he was left to be beaten by the sacristan mayor and Padre Salvi.
  • Seeing he was never seen again after this, it would be best to assume he was killed by the aforementioned.
Pilosopo Tasyo
  • Don Anastacio is Sisa's neighbor and is known as the lunatic of San Diego because he was smarter than all of them.
  • Every year, he tries to find his wife's skull despite knowing it is already gone.
  • He is the one who advises Ibarra with wisdom from time to time.
Elias
  • He is a fugitive in San Diego that seeks to improve his country.
  • After Ibarra saved his life, he has been a loyal friend and ally of his, despite them clashing with their morals.
Donya Victorina de Espadana
  • She is the wife of Don Tiburcio and a friend of Kapitan Tiago.
  • Shr prioritizes status over all else and pretends to be a Spaniard, despite being a native Filipino.
Don Tiburcio de Espadana
  • He is the husband of Donya Victorina and is a quack doctor.
  • In truth, he just pretends to be a doctor to keep his status.
  • He is also submissive to his wife.
